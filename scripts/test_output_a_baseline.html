Scientists use models to understand how Earth's materials cycle through different processes. In this activity, you will construct and analyze models of the rock cycle to explain how rocks transform from one type to another. Use evidence from the models to support your explanations.
1.WORKED EXAMPLE: Look at this rock cycle model. A sedimentary rock undergoes heat and pressure deep underground. The heat and pressure cause the rock's minerals to change and form new crystals without melting. What type of rock forms? Answer: Metamorphic rock. The process of heat and pressure changing a rock's structure without melting is called metamorphism, which creates metamorphic rocks.
